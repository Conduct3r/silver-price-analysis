# Silver Price Analysis (2016-2026)

Despite the recent selloff of silver on 1/30/26 which saw the price drop around 30%, it's hard to ignore that the commodity performed extremely well, reaching an all-time high of roughly $116. Its performance warranted significant news coverage, which got me thinking about the historical stability of silver prices.

## Questions explored:
1. What years were prices most volatile?
    - 2025 was the most volatile year in regards to prices with a daily average range of $0.77. 
    This is nearly double the volatility that was present in 2024 and 2020 at $0.42. 
2. How volatile has January been for silver compared to the last 10 years?
    - In January of 2026, the volatility of silver was about $3.97. This volatility is the largest amongst
    in the last 10 years by a substancial amount.
