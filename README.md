# Silver Price Analysis (2016-2026)

Despite the recent selloff of silver on 1/30/26 which saw the price drop around 30%, it's hard to ignore that the commodity performed extremely well, reaching an all-time high of roughly $116. Its performance warranted significant news coverage, which got me thinking about the historical stability of silver prices.

## Questions explored:
- What years were prices most volatile?
- What has been the typical price range for silver in the last 10 years?
- How does the forecast for Q1 2026 compare to Q1 2025?

## Tools:
- Python, pandas, matplotlib, seaborn

## Dataset:
[Silver Price Analysis and Forecast (2016-2026)](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/ashrafkhetran/silver-price-analysis-and-forecast-2016-2026)
